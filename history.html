FACTS/HISTORY

::HISTORY OF THE ISS

With its first component launched in 1988, the ISS has been in operation for 20 years, and is expected to operate until atleast 2028. The first module of the ISS was called Zarya, created by Russia. Originally intended to couple with a Russian space station, Zarya was used as a power generator and storage block during the preliminary construction on the ISS. Today, the ISS consists of 16 pressurized modules originating from three countires, with one European module. These modules' roles vary: some focus on research, some are intended for cargo, and some are used for human living.

  • -The ISS makes 16 orbits around Earth in 24 hours.
